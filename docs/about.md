# About FreeSTAR Everywhere

FreeSTAR Everywhere is a Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) service, designed exclusively for licensed amateur radio operators. It allows these operators to connect to the global amateur radio network using a standard telephone or any SIP-compatible device (like a smartphone app or VoIP phone).

## Mission

The mission of FreeSTAR Everywhere is to provide licensed amateur radio operators with seamless and accessible connectivity to the global FreeSTAR Network, utilizing common communication methods like telephone and VoIP services to bridge the gap between traditional and internet-based ham radio communication.
